EXCLUSIVE: Ciara &amp; Russell Wilson Are Having ANOTHER Baby!!

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Ciara and Russell Wilson are expecting another child, MTO News has confirmed. And we have video proof that Cici is pregnant again.

Two weeks ago, Ciara, 36, guest hosted The Ellen DeGeneres Show and was joined by her husband for the special occasion. During the show, the NFL quarterback, 33, surprised the “Level Up” singer with an unexpected proposal, asking his wife if she wanted to have another baby. 

The couple are already parents to 19-month-old son Win Harrison, daughter Sienna Princess, 4, as well as Russell’s step-son Future Zahir, 7, whom Ciara shares with ex-boyfriend, Future.

Ciara joked to the audience that her husband makes her extremely nervous, blushing after he gave her a large bouquet of roses.

The NFL star laughed and asked the audience, “Do you guys want me to make her more nervous?” as Ciara looked surprised. Dropping to one knee, Wilson asked, “I have a question for you. Serious question. Can we have more babies?”

Well – Russell already knew the answer to the question was yes. Because a fan caught up with Ciara yesterday and she had a noticeable baby bump that she was trying to hide.
