Yaritza Miguelina Reyes Ramirez is popularly known as Yaritza Reyes is a Dominican actress, singer, model, and beauty pageant titleholder. In this Spanish name, the first or paternal surname is Reyes and the second or maternal family name is Ramirez.

She was crowned Miss Dominican Republic 2013 and represented the Dominican Republic at Miss Universe 2013 in Moscow, Russia. She also won Miss Mundo Dominicana 2016 and subsequently represented the Dominican Republic in Miss World 2016 in Washington, D.C., United States.
