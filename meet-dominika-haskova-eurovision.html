Publish date: 2024-08-11

Meet Dominika Hašková Eurovision – Dominika Haskova is a member of the electropop duo We Are Domi and the Czech Republic’s representative at the Eurovision Song Contest in 2022. Learn more about the singer’s age and partner.

We Are Domi is made up of Diminika Haskova on lead vocals, Casper Hatlestad on guitar, and Benjamin Rekstad on keyboards. The band was founded in 2016 while studying at Leeds College of Music in Leeds, United Kingdom.

The Eurovision Song Contest 2022 is being held in Turin, Italy, after the country won the competition in 2021 with the song Zitti e buoni by Maneskin. The semi-finals will be held on May 10th and 12th, 2022, with the grand final scheduled for May 14th.

The band was originally formed as a one-time project for Haskova’s examination concert. Now they have released several singles and are set for a wider recognition following their entry in the contest. Here are more details about the lead singer of the electropop band.

Meet Dominika Haskova Eurovision

Dominika Hašková is trending in the music circle following the selection of her band to represent the Czech Republic in the 2022 Eurovision Song Contest. She is the voice behind the single ‘Lights Off‘ that was performed at the semi-final of the competition.

At the age of 15, Dominika took part in the first season of the talent show Česko Slovensko má talent in 2010. She won the judges’ vote in the third semi-final and was one of the 14 acts selected for the grand final. Though impressing the judges and audiences, she failed to win the competition, which was won by the acrobalance act Dae Men.

We Are Domi was formed by the trio and included the British keyboardist and backing vocalist Jemma Freese and Norwegian drummer Jostein Braaten, who later left the band. The trio continued making music together and relocated to Prague, Czech Republic from the United Kingdom.

How Old Is Dominika Haskova Age And Partner Details

The singer was born on May 3, 1995, in Buffalo, New York, United States while her father, ice hockey legend Dominik Hašek was playing for the Buffalo Sabres in the NHL.

Hašek is considered one of the best goaltenders of all time. He mostly played in the NHL for the Buffalo Sabres, where he earned the nickname ‘The Dominator’. He also has a son named Mike Hasek.

There are no details on the partner of the We Are Domi singer. She likes to keep her personal life out of the limelight. Though she has posted pictures of her with her bandmates, Casper and Benjamin, the Czech singer has not posted any pictures of herself with her partner or boyfriend.

The Czech Republic On Eurovision 2022

The Czech Republic had competed nine times prior to the 2022 contest. Its first entry was in 2007. Three acts have reached the finals, the best of which was the sixth-place finish by the singer Mikolas Josef. We Are Domi are aiming to at least finish higher than the previous best placing.

We Are Domi was selected through the Eurovision Song CZ 2022, the national final organized by the CT. Among the seven participants, the electropop trio was the one selected to represent the country in the semi-finals.
