Camilla Luddington Debuts New Blonde Hair Ahead of Greys Anatomy Season 18 See the Pic!

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Camilla Luddington is feeling her new look!

The 37-year-old actress debuted her freshly bleached blonde hair in a selfie posted to Instagram on Wednesday (September 15), two weeks before the new season of Grey’s Anatomy premieres.

“………Season 18,” she wrote in the caption, along with the hashtags “#JoWilson #blonde #NewEra.”

Sarah Drew commented, ” “Wowowowowowowo????,” while Jake Borelli wrote, “The TEA!”
