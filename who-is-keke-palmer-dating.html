Who is Keke Palmer dating?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

KEKE Palmer is well known as an outspoken celebrity who shares a lot about herself on social media.

Nevertheless, she seems to be silent about her current rumored relationship.

Who is Keke Palmer dating?

As of March 2022, Keke Palmer was reportedly dating actor, Darius Daulton, also known as Darius Jackson.

The pair reportedly met at Diddy and Issa Rae’s Memorial Day Party in May 2021.

Speaking to Bustle about her relationship in March 2022, she stated: “This is the happiest I’ve ever felt with someone. So why would I go out of my way to hide this person?

“That’s a lot more work than just living in my life and being in my life.”

However, Keke seems to have deleted every post about Darius from her Instagram page. Furthermore, he has also deleted his Instagram profile.

As a result, it is unclear what their current relationship status is.

Since the two have not publicly commented on their relationship status, it is possible that they have either made their relationship private, or they have broken up.

Who is Keke Palmer?

Lauren Keyana Palmer, popularly known as Keke Palmer, was born on August 26, 1993, in Harvey, Illinois.

Keke is a versatile actress, television personality, and singer.

Keke made her acting debut in 2004 when she starred in Barbershop 2: Back in Business.

In the same year, she appeared in the television film The Wool Cap.

She also made a breakthrough when she starred in the drama film, Akeelah and the Bee.

Concerning her music career, she released her debut studio album, So Uncool, in 2007.

As a result of her stellar performances, she has received a Primetime Emmy Award, five NAACP Image Awards, and nominations for a Daytime Emmy Award, and a Screen Actors Guild Award.

Additionally, Time magazine listed her as one of the most influential people in the world in 2019.

What is Keke Palmer’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Keke Palmer has an estimated net worth of approximately $7.5million, as of July 2022.

Her main source of wealth is said to be from her career in the entertainment industry as an actress, singer, and television personality.

She also earns income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.
