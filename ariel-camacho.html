Ariel Camacho or Jose Ariel Camacho Barraza was a Mexican who was a popular Mexican singer and songwriter by profession. His band, Ariel Camacho y Los Plebes del Rancho, was signed to JG Record and subsequently DEL Records, and he was the band’s leader.

He majorly worked on the genre Regional Mexican and has worked with the labels JG Music, and DEL Records. He majorly played the vocals and the Requinto. He was active in the industry from the year 2012 to the year 2015.

Ariel Camacho Wiki/Biography

Born on 08 July 1992 and died on 25 February 2015, Ariel Camacho’s age was only 22 years at the time of his death. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Guamuchil, Sinaloa, Mexico. He is a Mexican by nationality and has his belief in the Christianity religion.

He completed his early schooling at a Local Elementary School, in Guamuchil, Sinaloa, Mexico.

After that, he chose to form a music band of his own in the year 2013. The name of his band was Los Plebes del Rancho.
