Jennifer Lawrence 'gives birth to first child' with husband Cooke Maroney

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Jennifer Lawrence has reportedly given birth to her first child with husband Cooke Maroney.

According to TMZ, the Academy Award-winner welcomed her baby in Los Angeles county but no gender or exact birthdate has been revealed.

DailyMail.com has reached out to reps for the actress for comment who have so far been unavailable. 

Lawrence and Cooke got married in 2019 during an intimate ceremony in Rhode Island after a whirlwind romance.
