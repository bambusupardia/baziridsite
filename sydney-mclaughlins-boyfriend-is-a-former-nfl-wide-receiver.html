Sydney McLaughlins Boyfriend Is A Former NFL Wide Receiver

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Sydney McLaughlin is best known for winning gold in the women’s 400m hurdles at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics with a time of 51.46 seconds. She also won silver at the World Championships in 2019. She was also the first woman to breach the 52-second barrier when she broke the world record of 51.90s in the 2020 USA Olympic trials. McLaughlin is in a great relationship with her lover, according to her personal life. She even got proposed to her fiancé in August 2021 after he dropped down on one knee in an outdoor location surrounded by bouquets of flowers and candles.

Sydney McLaughlin’s boyfriend used to be a wide receiver in the NFL.

Andre Levrone Jr., a former NFL wide receiver, is the two-time Olympic gold medalist’s boyfriend. The pair got engaged at the Four Seasons Resort Scottsdale in Arizona, and the couple announced the news on social media shortly after. McLaughlin addressed her partner as her “future husband” in her Instagram post, which included a succession of the couple’s photos and long yet emotional comments. “I still can’t believe how someone who has everything I’ve wished for has finally come into my life,” she said in the post.

She went on to say that she was becoming more and more in love with him every day. Her now-fiancé, Levrone Jr., was the most God-fearing, passionate, honest, loving, hard-working, and protective guy she had ever encountered, according to her. McLaughlin and Levrone Jr have apparently been dating since 2020. In December of that year, they initially disclosed their connection by posting a photo of themselves on Instagram.

Sydney McLaughlin’s Parents were Athletes Too!

McLaughlin’s parents, William and Mary McLaughlin, were also athletes, much like her and her partner. William or Willie, the hurdler’s father, was a semi-finalist in the 400m at the 1984 Olympic trials. Her mother, Mary, was a high school runner who specialized in the 400m and 800m. Taylor McLaughlin, McLaughlin’s elder brother, had also won silver in the 400m hurdles at the 2016 IAAF World U20 Championships. The Olympic gold medallist was born on August 7, 1999, in Dunellen, New Jersey, to an elder sister, Morgan, an older brother, Taylor, and a younger brother, Ryan.

Willie complimented his son and daughter for their outstanding high school results in an interview with a local internet media outlet. “We aren’t astonished by what Taylor and Sydney have done,” he said, adding, “but they’re still just 16 and 14, so we want to make sure they’re not putting pressure on themselves.” We are quite proud of them.”

Sydney McLaughlin’s Ethnic Origins

McLaughlin’s heritage is African American, according to different web publications, despite the fact that she has not publicly stated it in public. Her father is supposedly African American, while her mother is of European ethnicity. McLaughlin is also of American nationality since she was born and reared in the United States.
