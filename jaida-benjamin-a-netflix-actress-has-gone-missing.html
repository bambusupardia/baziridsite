Jaida Benjamin A Netflix Actress Has Gone Missing

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Jaida Benjamin, who portrays Kelly in Netflix’s Family Reunion, has gone missing, according to reports. According to the Los Angeles Police Department, the actress was last seen on February 19, 2022, near Tujunga Avenue and Ventura Boulevard in the Los Angeles area. She was last seen with her hair pulled back and dressed in a purple crop top, pink jogging shorts, and white shoes. Her family has been unable to reach her since she was last seen, and the LAPD thinks there is no foul play.

Jocinda Benjamin, her mother, begged for public assistance in an Instagram post:

“I never imagined I’d have to write a piece like this. My child has gone missing. Please assist me in locating her. I’m having trouble breathing.”

Jihan Johnston, a co-founder of Beat Botics, tweeted an update regarding Benjamin’s disappearance.

Jaida Benjamin has allegedly gone missing.

Jaida-Iman Benjamin is a dancer and actress from North Carolina, USA. She is very new, yet she has already earned a reputation for herself in Hollywood. The actress is most recognized for her appearances in The Step Daddy, Southland, Mad About You, and Christmas With My Ex, all of which she shared with actors Denise Boutte and Travis Cure. She’s also been in films, including Christmas Everlasting, Murder in the Vineyard, and Life of a King. Her performance as Rhonda in the Cuba Gooding, Jr. film Life of a King gained critical acclaim. She has appeared in Family Reunion, Liv and Maddie, The Fosters, Insecure, Stuck in the Middle, Austin & Ally, and Foursome. In addition, the actor had a minor part in the television film Halloween Special: Bad Things, Good Things.

How to Contact the Los Angeles Police Department for More Information on Jaida Benjamin

Those with information about Jaida Benjamin’s whereabouts are urged to contact the Los Angeles Police Department’s Missing Persons Unit. They may call the Los Angeles Regional Crime Stoppers if they choose to remain anonymous. This is the second similar case this month; on February 13, 2022, actress Lindsey Pearlman of General Hospital was reported missing after last being seen in Hollywood. She was found dead on February 18, 2022. The coroner has yet to identify the cause of death.
