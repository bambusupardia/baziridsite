Full 'Borderlands' Movie Cast Revealed After Final Casting Announcement!

Publish date: 2024-08-17

The highly anticipated movie Borderlands, an adaptation of the popular video game, has completed casting and you can see the full cast list here!

Eli Roth is directing the upcoming movie for Lionsgate and Cate Blanchett is starring as the lead role of Lilith.

Roth told Deadline, “I am so excited to work with this incredible, top notch cast. Every single part counts and elevates the film and we have the best of the best coming in to really make Borderlands spectacular. I love movies where every character counts and you can only achieve that with a great cast. Every one of these gifted actors will bring something special to the film.”

The new casting additions including Gina Gershon and Cheyenne Jackson, among others.
